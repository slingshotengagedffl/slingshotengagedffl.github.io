Boom, Start & Bust

Every week a manager either shows up or doesn't. A Boom is scoring 120% or more of that season's league average. A Start is scoring at or above average — boom weeks count. A Mid is below average but above 80% of average. A Bust is 80% or less of the season average. These are the scorecards.

Boom ≥120%
Start ≥100% (incl. booms)
Mid 80–100%
Bust ≤80%
